Rhode Island has a thriving tech ecosystem and it's growing stronger every day.

In order to really succeed as Brown CS students, we have tap into this growing local ecosystem. That's where Bears@Work comes in. We connect you with local tech in year round semester based internships where you get:

  • Real world coding experience
  • Personal connections with RI tech founders
  • $15 - $25/hour
